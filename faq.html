Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the E-Visa application process, document requirements, and more.

Application Process

Register an account on our portal, then click "New Application" on your dashboard. Fill in your personal details, select visa type, upload required documents, and submit. You will receive a reference number instantly.

We currently support Tourist Visa, Business Visa, Student Visa, Work Permit, and Family Visa. Each visa type has specific requirements listed during the application process.

Standard processing takes 3-5 business days. Express processing (48 hours) is available for an additional fee. You will be notified via email once a decision is made.

Once submitted, applications cannot be edited. If you made an error, please contact our support team immediately with your reference number. In some cases, a new application may be required.

Documents & Upload

Required documents include: valid passport copy (minimum 6 months validity), passport-size photograph, proof of accommodation, travel itinerary, and bank statements (last 3 months). Additional documents may be required based on visa type.

We accept PDF, JPG, and PNG formats. Each file must not exceed 5MB in size. Ensure documents are clear, legible, and not password-protected before uploading.

Yes. All documents are encrypted and stored on secure government-grade servers. Your data is only accessible to authorized immigration officers processing your application.

Application Status

Log in to your account and visit the Dashboard. Your application status is shown in real time. Statuses include: Submitted, In Review, Approved, and Rejected. You will also receive email notifications for every status change.

Submitted — Application received. In Review — Being processed by an officer. Approved — Visa granted. Rejected — Application was not approved; you may reapply.

If your application is rejected, you will receive a notification with the reason. You may reapply after addressing the issues mentioned. Contact our support team for guidance on reapplication.

Account & Login

Click "Apply Online" or "Register" on the login page. Enter your full name, email address, and create a password. Your account will be created instantly and you can start applying right away.

Currently, please contact our support team at support@evisa.gov.pk with your registered email address to reset your password. A password reset feature will be available soon.

Yes. You can submit multiple visa applications under a single account. All applications will appear in your dashboard with their individual reference numbers and status.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. We typically respond within 24 hours.
